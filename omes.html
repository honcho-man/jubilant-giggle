What drives our team?

An Experienced and Teamwork Orientated Engineering Company

Our team of electrical contractors are highly focused on ensuring your property is respected and cleaned. We have the tools and electrical supplies to keep your property tidy from start to finish including; painters drop sheets, vacuum cleaners, boot covers and more.

We have a range of stocked vehicles in order to complete jobs in the same visit and reduce the overall customer cost. We also employ a full time Workshop and Purchasing Officer who can deliver standard items, which may be required to complete the job without having to re-book further appointments.

We use a service tracking system which enables us to locate our tradesmen at any time to help our clients with urgent jobs and offer faster response times. Our Service Manager is dedicated to controlling the daily jobs to ensure they run smoothly.
